# Return and Refund Policy

## Return Window
- Customers can request a return within 7 calendar days from delivery date.
- Product must be unused, with tags/labels intact, and in original packaging.
- Innerwear, personalized products, and final-sale products are not returnable.

## Refund Eligibility
- Full refund is processed after quality check confirms return conditions.
- If product is damaged during transit, customer can claim refund/replacement within 48 hours of delivery.
- Refund can be rejected when product is used, missing accessories, or does not match return reason.

## Refund Timeline
- Prepaid orders: refund to original payment method in 5-7 business days after quality check.
- COD orders: refund to bank account or wallet in 5-10 business days after account validation.

## Exchange
- Exchange is allowed once per order item, subject to stock availability.
- Size/color exchange is supported for apparel and footwear.

## Cancellation
- Order can be cancelled before shipment confirmation.
- Once shipped, cancellation is not guaranteed; customer should place a return request after delivery.

## Support
- Contact support at support@aiecommerce.demo with Order ID and issue details.
